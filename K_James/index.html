Short Biography

A biography can have exciting approaches and be told or written in many ways, which is why every biography is unique to that person. This is the very concept that I would like to use to describe myself because often, my life takes exciting approaches, but I am a unique person. I am Kaylani James, a short, curly-headed girl with a light complexion. In my country or background, they would call me a “shabin” or “coolie,” but in America, I am light skin. My personality is driven by my culture, which derives from a country named Saint Lucia in the West Indies. A Caribbean girl who is afraid to talk most times and doesn’t come off friendly, but when comfortable, is the life of the party.

If you see me walking without my phone in my hand or hearing me sing a song, please be aware that something is wrong or I’ve lost it. My anxiety drives me insane constantly to the point where I avoid human interaction and look at my phone. Although, the power of music overwhelms everything in me and return, produces a voice that everyone loves to hear. Whether it be an R&B song from the 2000s to a rap or afrobeat song from the present day, know I’m singing it. My stubbornness continues as it allows me to fight for anything I truly believe. My books create a fantasy world that will enable me to live a life that the campus hasn’t begun yet.

As a summer B student, I made my lifelong college best friend, who is not my roommate anymore but someone that made me a better person. She was the person I went to the cafe the first time with, and I learned about the campus and parts of Tallahassee. Although I am still trying to put myself out there and break out of my shell, if someone could give me a chance and get to know me, you will always see my heart of gold. Or, visit my love for technology through games because I can go on about all types and how I always win or try to. Ultimately, I’m a determined, motivated person who always loves to crack a joke behind the crowd.
